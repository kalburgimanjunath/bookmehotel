Welcome to Rajmahal Comforts

Rajmahal Comforts, a homely boarding and lodging destination by Arjun Production House Pvt. Ltd., strives to provide the best hospitality services. Rajmahal Comforts, a budget hotel provides excellent value based lodging facilities to travellers. Satisfaction of customers is the prime focus of Rajmahal Comforts. Backed with the motto for excellence, Rajmahal Comforts strives to provide the best services in facility maintenance and management.

At Rajmahal Comforts, accommodation is available according to your requirement and budget. The lodge is spread over three floors with six rooms on each floor. Both AC and Non-AC rooms are available at varying tariff with all the facilities. We at Rajmahal Comforts, ensure the traveller’s stay is made delightful and a memorable one by offering a host of finest services. Rajmahal Comforts is an ideal choice for the populace of North Bangalore.
